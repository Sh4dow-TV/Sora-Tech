Core Freight Solutions

Whether you’re sending stock for your business, personal items to family, or building a new life between Nigeria and the UK — our freight options are designed to match your budget, speed and cargo size.

Sea Freight

Sea Freight Forwarding

Reliable, cost-effective ocean shipping for bulk and containerised goods across international waters.

Ideal for: Large volumes, non-urgent deliveries, imports/exports.

Mode: Sea · Container / LCL

Air Freight

Air Freight Forwarding

Fast and secure delivery for urgent or high-value shipments with priority handling and scheduled flights.

Ideal for: Time-sensitive parcels, high-value goods, perishables.

Mode: Air · Express / Standard

Road Freight

Road Freight & Local Delivery

Flexible, efficient road transport for domestic and cross-border deliveries with door-to-door coverage.

Ideal for: Local distribution, last-mile delivery, regional cargo.

Mode: Road · Van / Truck

Door-to-Door & Specialty Services

Door-to-Door

Door-to-Door (Nigeria ↔ UK)

End-to-end service including pickup, customs clearance, and delivery directly to your recipient’s door.

Perfect for: Diaspora families, small businesses, personal shipments.

Consolidation

Cargo Consolidation

Combine multiple parcels into one shipment to save on costs and simplify customs and delivery.

Perfect for: Frequent shippers, e-commerce sellers, SMEs.

Express

Express Priority Shipping

Fastest possible routing for critical shipments, handled with top-priority support and tracking.

Perfect for: Urgent documents, samples, important deliveries.
